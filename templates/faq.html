Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Dareora.

What is Dareora?

Dareora is a platform for college students to challenge each other with dares, complete them, and share their experiences. It's all about stepping out of your comfort zone and creating fun, memorable moments.

How do I submit a dare?

You can submit a dare by clicking the "Submit a Dare" button on the homepage or navigating to the "Dare App" and finding the create button. You'll need to fill out a form with details like the dare title, description, category, and difficulty.

Are there any rules for submitting dares?

Yes. Dares cannot contain content related to hate, violence, illegal activities, or drugs. All submissions are reviewed, and dares that violate these rules will be rejected.

How are dares approved?

Our team reviews each submitted dare to ensure it's safe, fun, and follows our community guidelines. You will receive a notification once your dare is approved.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes. Information like your phone number is used for verification purposes only and is not displayed publicly on the site.

What are the dare categories?

We have four main categories: Extreme, Creative, Social, and Adventure, each designed to cater to different kinds of dare-devil spirits.
